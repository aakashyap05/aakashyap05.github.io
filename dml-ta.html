Lab Work

Design and Manufacturing Lab Teaching Assistant

As a Design and Manufacturing Lab teaching assistant, I support students working through hands-on engineering, manufacturing, design, prototyping, and equipment-based lab activities.

Responsibilities

  • Assist students with design and manufacturing lab activities.
  • Support safe and effective use of lab equipment and tools.
  • Help troubleshoot student projects and manufacturing challenges.
  • Explain technical concepts in a clear and practical way.

Tools and Skills

Manufacturing, prototyping, lab support, troubleshooting, technical instruction, student guidance.

Engineering Skills Developed

  • Communicating technical ideas to students with different experience levels.
  • Helping diagnose design, manufacturing, and assembly issues.
  • Building confidence with hands-on engineering tools and lab workflows.
  • Balancing safety, practicality, and engineering problem solving.

What I Learned

This role strengthens both my technical and leadership skills because it requires me to explain engineering concepts clearly while helping students work through real design and manufacturing challenges.
